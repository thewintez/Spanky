The Proprietary Blend

Three ingredients.
One very good reason.

Traditional aphrodisiac botanicals, used for centuries across three continents, now baked into the only cracker that earns its place on a nightstand.

Maca Root
Lepidium meyenii · Peruvian Highlands

Used by Incan warriors for endurance and vitality before battle. We're not suggesting anything. We're just noting the historical context.

Damiana
Turnera diffusa · Central Mexico

A wild shrub with centuries of use as a mood-lifter and nerve tonic. The Aztecs called it something we can't print here. The effect speaks for itself.

Horny Goat Weed
Epimedium · Yunnan Province, China

Named after an elderly goat farmer's rather inconvenient observation. Over 2,000 years in Chinese medicine. The name is unfortunate. The results are not.
